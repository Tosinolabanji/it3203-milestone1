Home — Abstract & Site Map

Abstract: Web servers are the backbone of the modern web — software and hardware that respond to HTTP/HTTPS requests and deliver content to users. This project surveys the evolution of servers, with special focus on Microsoft Internet Information Services (IIS) and how it fits alongside Apache, Nginx, and modern cloud-native patterns.
