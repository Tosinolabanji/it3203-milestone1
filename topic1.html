History & Evolution of Web Servers

This section summarizes the early web server milestones and how architectures evolved.

Timeline

YearMilestone
1990CERN httpd (Tim Berners-Lee)
1993NCSA HTTPd and early public websites
1995Apache becomes dominant
2004Nginx released (event-driven, high concurrency)

Comparison (brief)

  • Apache: Modular, open-source, process-driven historically.
  • IIS: Windows-integrated, GUI admin, enterprise adoption.
  • Nginx: Event-driven, reverse-proxy & load-balancer strengths.
